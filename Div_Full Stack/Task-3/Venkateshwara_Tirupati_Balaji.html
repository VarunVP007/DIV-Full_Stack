About the Temple

The Venkateshwara Temple in Tirumala is one of the richest and most visited temples dedicated to Lord Venkateshwara (a form of Vishnu).

It is called the "Spiritual Capital" for devotees across India.

The temple is known for its grand architecture and the spiritual experience it offers to millions of pilgrims.
