About the Temple

The Siddhivinayak Temple in Mumbai is a popular temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha, especially visited for blessings of success.

It is one of the richest temples in Mumbai.

The temple has a magnificent golden dome and a highly revered Ganesha idol.
