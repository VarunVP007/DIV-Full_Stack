About the Temple

The Ramanathaswamy Temple is a famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva located in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, India.

It is one of the twelve Jyotirlinga temples and holds great religious significance for Hindus.

The temple is known for its longest corridor among all Hindu temples, and the architectural beauty is truly breathtaking.
