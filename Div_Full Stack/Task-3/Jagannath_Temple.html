About the Temple

The Jagannath Temple in Puri is a sacred Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Jagannath (Krishna/Vishnu avatar).

It is famous for its annual Ratha Yatra (chariot festival).

The temple features a unique style of worship and has a rich mythological history.
