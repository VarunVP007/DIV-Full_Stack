About the Temple

The Somnath Temple is one of the twelve Jyotirlinga shrines of Lord Shiva, located on the western coast of Gujarat.

It symbolizes the resilience of India's culture through multiple reconstructions.

The temple's seaside location and its grand architecture make it very special.
